Meet the Founder

Professional Care & Empathy

Beth Awuor is a counselling psychology professional and wellness community facilitator passionate about creating safe spaces for mental and emotional well-being. She is dedicated to helping individuals navigate life’s challenges, build resilience, and feel seen and heard. Recognizing that many people suffer in silence—often unaware that they need support—Beth is committed to breaking the stigma around mental health and making conversations more open and accessible. Through community sessions, one-on-one support, and group engagements, she promotes healing, growth, and self-awareness. She is also exploring the integration of technology into mental health, with a vision to create digital tools that support emotional well-being and connect people to care.
